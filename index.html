About Us

About the Church

Kingdom Calibration Church is an Apostolic and Prophetic ministry headquartered in Westlands Nairobi with one of its key mandates being to reach the Asian community, and to RECONCILE professional nations of influence (7 nations) back to Christ.

Vision Statement
“
"To Transform Lives and All Things By Aligning Them Back To God's Eternal Purpose"

Colossians 1:19-20

Mission Statement

We are centered on teaching the gospel of the Kingdom to all men, with an emphasis on healing, deliverance and equipping men for their Kingdom assignment, and demonstrating the power of God.

Healing. Deliverance. Equipping. Demonstration.
Stewards of the Vision

Apostolic Leadership

Apostle Boniface Nagi

Apostle Boniface is the visionary leader and foundational voice behind Kingdom Calibration Church, a ministry birthed by the Holy Spirit to raise, equip, and send believers into their God-given assignments. Called by God from humble beginnings, Apostle Boniface carries a strong apostolic mandate to plant, build, and establish a healthy church grounded on truth, order, and the power of the Holy Spirit.

Prophetess Joyce Wangechi

While the Apostle focuses on teaching, structure, and laying strong foundations, the Prophetess covers the ministry in prayer, intercession, and spiritual discernment. She serves as a watchman, sensing spiritual atmospheres, offering prophetic guidance, and helping keep the church aligned with the voice of God. Her support strengthens the Apostle and creates an environment where the Holy Spirit moves freely.

Apostle's Journey

Boniface Nagi

An apostolic story marked by calling, foundation-laying, and a burden to see lives aligned with the eternal purpose of God.

Apostle Boniface is presented not simply as a leader of a church, but as a man carrying the weight of a house, a people, and a clear kingdom assignment.

A call from humble beginnings

His story is rooted in obedience. From humble beginnings, he has carried a growing conviction that God was not only shaping his life, but preparing him to raise, equip, and send others into their own assignments.

Building with truth and order

The grace on his life is apostolic. It shows in the desire to plant, build, and establish a healthy church grounded in truth, order, and the power of the Holy Spirit rather than noise, pressure, or performance.

A burden for alignment

At the center of his journey is a burden to see believers aligned with divine purpose. His leadership carries both vision and structure, calling people beyond attendance into maturity, assignment, and kingdom authority.

"Apostolic leadership, for him, is not about position. It is about stewarding people into the purposes of God." Stewardship, vision, foundation
Prophetess' Journey

Joyce Wangechi

A story shaped in the secret place, carried through intercession, and expressed with prophetic sensitivity, healing grace, and spiritual discernment.

Prophetess Joyce carries a ministry that feels deeply personal and prayer-soaked, shaped more by intimacy with the Spirit than by outward display.

Formed in the place of prayer

Her journey reflects a life hidden with God. The strength of her ministry flows from prayer, intercession, and a watchful sensitivity that discerns what the Spirit is saying in the life of the church.

The grace of discernment and healing

She is known for prophetic precision, healing, and spiritual attentiveness. That grace helps people feel seen, covered, and shepherded with gentleness as well as conviction.

Keeping the house aligned

Her role is deeply strategic even when it appears quiet. She serves as a watchman over atmospheres, offering prophetic guidance and helping preserve an environment where the Holy Spirit can move freely.

"Her support is not secondary. It is foundational to the prayer, discernment, and spiritual covering of the house." Prayer, prophecy, intimacy
Their Journey Together

One Stewardship,
Two Graces

Their shared story is one of complement, not competition: structure and discernment, teaching and prayer, foundation and atmosphere.

Their journey together is not simply about serving alongside one another. It is about stewarding one mandate through two distinct but deeply complementary graces.

Different expressions, one burden

Where Apostle Boniface carries structure, teaching, and apostolic foundation, Prophetess Joyce brings prayer, discernment, and prophetic covering. Their differences do not divide the work. They deepen it.

A house covered in both order and prayer

Together they help shape a ministry environment where the Word is taught clearly, the Spirit is honoured fully, and people are invited into both formation and encounter.

A shared witness

Their partnership tells its own story: a witness of stewardship, agreement, and endurance. It reflects a shared commitment to calibrate lives, strengthen families, and see believers restored to God's eternal purpose.

"Together they steward a house where truth, prayer, healing, and alignment can flourish side by side." Shared mandate, shared stewardship
